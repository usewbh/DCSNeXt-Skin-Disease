# DCSNeXt-Skin-Disease
Dual-modality deep learning–based skin disease diagnosis and Physician–AI collaborative evaluation: multi-source validation and exploration of clinical applicability.
Our intelligent skin lesion–assisted diagnostic platform can be accessed via the following link: http://wengzq-lab.cn/AI-Skin/

A systematic framework was established to integrate multi-source data curation, model development and evaluation, interpretability analyses, Physician–AI collaborative validation, and two-center external validation with robustness testing. Dermoscopic and clinical images were collected from public databases, literature, and two local hospitals in Fuzhou and Chongqing, China. First, DCSNeXt, a novel diagnostic model, was developed on public datasets and systematically compared with representative models. Second, a dual-reference framework incorporating Model and Physician Ensembles was established for comparison with DCSNeXt on literature-derived datasets. Third, multi-perspective interpretability analyses elucidated the model’s decision rationale. Fourth, a collaborative diagnostic paradigm integrating model predictions, saliency heatmaps, and Physician–AI interaction was designed and validated, with external testing and simulated stress assessments confirming robustness.
